That Time I Swam Across the Hudson River

As a Stevens student, I see the Hudson River almost every day. I've been told you can find dead bodies in it and that if you took a swim in it you'd grow an extra toe. But if that's true, why do the little swimming ducks seem to be enjoying themselves so much?

One day the water looked too nice to resist. It was late and dark, so there was no one around to judge me. I went to the beach area at Maxwell Park and swam out. It was so refreshing! I floated on my back and looked up at the city. It looked so beautiful, as did its reflection on the water.

That's my secret. And I still haven't grown an extra toe!
